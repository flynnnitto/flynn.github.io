// 01 — About

Who I Am

I'm a game designer with a focus on systems design, level design, and player psychology. I love finding the sweet spot between challenge and flow — where players lose track of time.

My background spans mobile, indie, and jam games. I'm comfortable working solo or as part of a small team, and I care deeply about design documentation and clear communication.

When I'm not designing, I'm usually dissecting other games, sketching mechanics on paper, or losing sleep over a puzzle I can't quite solve.
